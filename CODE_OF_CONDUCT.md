# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

SkyWalker is an open, welcoming project. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Sharing knowledge and helping others get unstuck
- Giving constructive feedback on PRs and issues
- Crediting others' work and ideas
- Disagreeing respectfully on technical approaches

**Not tolerated:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately misleading others
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any other channel where you represent the project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to [projects@edoborosasere.com](mailto:projects@edoborosasere.com). Reports will be reviewed confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code, and may ban contributors who repeatedly violate it.
